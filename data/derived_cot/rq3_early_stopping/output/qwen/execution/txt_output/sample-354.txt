- **Size:** The `size` field is `'19'`, interpreted as the target length of the symlink.
- **Name:** The `name` field is `'debian'` (after stripping the link target `-> ./pub/mirror/debian`).
- **is_dir:** Set to `true` because it's a symlink (due to `perms.startswith('l')`).
- **FTP:** The entire input line is stored under the `"ftp"` key for reference.
