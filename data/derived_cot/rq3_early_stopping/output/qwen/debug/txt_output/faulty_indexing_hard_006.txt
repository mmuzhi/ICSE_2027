But in our `dp` function, the sub-pizza is from row i to self.r-1 and column j to self.c-1. So the bottom-right is (self.r-1, self.c-1). In the prefix sum array `self.pref`, the value at (self.r, self.c) is the entire pizza.

To compute the apples in the sub-pizza from (i, j) to (self.r-1, self.c-1):

   apples = self.pref[self.r][self.c] - self.pref[i][self.c] - self.pref[self.r][j] + self.pref[i][j]

But wait, the prefix sum array `self.pref` is defined such that `self.pref[i][j]` is the sum of the rectangle from (0,0) to (i-1, j-1).
