The left pointer starts from the next element, and the right pointer starts from the end of the array. We adjust the pointers based on whether the current sum is less than or greater than X.

4. **Complexity Analysis**: 
   - **Time Complexity**: O(N log N) for sorting the array and O(N^2) for the two-pointer approach in the worst case. Given the constraints, this might not be optimal for very large N, but it is a practical solution for many cases.
