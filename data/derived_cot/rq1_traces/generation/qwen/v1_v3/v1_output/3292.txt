2. **Check Feasibility for a Given Time**: For each candidate time `X`, we:
   - **Gather Candidates**: Collect all occurrences of each index in the first `X` elements of `changeIndices`.
   - **Check Presence**: Ensure each index appears at least once in the first `X` elements of `changeIndices`; otherwise, it's impossible to mark that index by time `X`.
   - **Sort by Decrement Requirement**: Process indices with higher decrement requirements first.
   - **Assign Marking Seconds**: For each index, assign the smallest candidate second that is at least the sum of its decrement requirement and the count of indices with equal or higher requirements. This ensures that the marking can be done after sufficient decrements.
