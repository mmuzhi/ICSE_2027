3. **Algorithm Selection**: The chosen algorithm uses the identity \( \lceil \frac{X}{10} \rceil = - \left\lfloor -\frac{X}{10} \right\rfloor \). This involves negating \( X \), performing floor division by 10, and then negating the result. This approach efficiently handles both positive and negative integers using integer arithmetic, avoiding precision issues.
