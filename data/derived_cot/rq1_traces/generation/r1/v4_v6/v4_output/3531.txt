=== ANSWER ===
To solve this problem, we need to minimize the total damage Bob takes from his enemies before all of them are defeated. The key insight is that the order in which Bob attacks the enemies affects the total damage, as each enemy contributes damage per second for every second they remain alive. The optimal strategy involves scheduling attacks on enemies in a way that minimizes the cumulative damage, considering both the damage each enemy deals and the time required to defeat them.

### Approach
1. **Calculate Time to Kill Each Enemy**: For each enemy, compute the number of attacks (seconds) Bob needs to defeat them. This is given by the ceiling of the enemy's health divided by Bob's attack power, i.e., `t_i = ceil(health[i] / power)`.
2. **Sort Enemies by Damage-to-Time Ratio**: To minimize the total damage, enemies should be prioritized based on their damage per unit time. Specifically, enemies with higher `damage[i] / t_i` ratios should be attacked first. This ensures that enemies causing more damage per second are eliminated earlier, reducing their overall contribution to the total damage.
3. **Compute Total Damage**: Using a greedy approach, process each enemy in the sorted order. For each enemy, accumulate the total time (prefix sum) taken to defeat all enemies processed so far. The damage contributed by each enemy is then `damage[i] * prefix_sum`, where `prefix_sum` is the cumulative time up to and including the time taken to defeat the current enemy.

### Solution Code
```python
import math

class Solution:
    def minDamage(self, power: int, damage: List[int], health: List[int]) -> int:
        n = len(damage)
        t = [(h + power - 1) // power for h in health]
        jobs = list(zip(damage, t))
        jobs.sort(key=lambda x: x[0] / x[1], reverse=True)
        prefix = 0
        total_damage = 0
        for w, t_val in jobs:
            prefix += t_val
            total_damage += w * prefix
        return total_damage
```

### Explanation
1. **Time Calculation**: For each enemy, the time `t_i` required to defeat them is calculated as the ceiling of their health divided by Bob's attack power. This gives the number of attacks needed to reduce the enemy's health to zero.
2. **Sorting Enemies**: The enemies are sorted based on their damage per unit time (`damage[i] / t_i`). This prioritizes enemies that deal more damage relative to the time needed to defeat them, ensuring they are eliminated first to minimize their damage contribution.
3. **Damage Accumulation**: As each enemy is processed in the sorted order, the cumulative time (prefix sum) is updated. The total damage is incremented by the product of the enemy's damage and the cumulative time, representing the damage dealt by the enemy over the seconds they remained alive.
4. **Result**: The accumulated total damage after processing all enemies is returned, which is the minimum possible damage Bob incurs by following the optimal attack strategy. This approach efficiently minimizes the total damage by leveraging a greedy algorithm based on the damage-to-time ratio.
