### Approach

2. **Making a Move**: A player places their symbol ('X' or 'O') in a specified cell (row, col). The move is valid only if the cell is empty. After a valid move, the current player switches.
3. **Checking Winner**: The winner is determined by checking all rows, columns, and diagonals in the top-left 3x3 subgrid (even if the board has more columns). The first player with three consecutive symbols wins.
4. **Checking Full Board**: The board is full if all cells (3 rows and N columns) are occupied by 'X' or 'O'.

#include <optional>

class TicTacToe {
private:
