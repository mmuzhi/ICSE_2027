Let's double check the Dutch National Flag algorithm:
- `low` pointer keeps track of the boundary of 0s.
- `mid` pointer is the current element being processed.
- `high` pointer keeps track of the boundary of 2s.
- Initial state: `low = 0`, `mid = 0`, `high = len(nums) - 1`.
- Loop condition: `while mid <= high`.

- If `nums[mid] == 1`: increment `mid`.
- If `nums[mid] == 2`: swap `nums[mid]` and `nums[high]`, decrement `high`. Do NOT increment `mid`.
