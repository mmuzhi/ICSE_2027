We iterate through the prices, adjusting our buy and sell points based on the current and next day's prices. If the next day's price is higher than the current sell price, we hold the stock longer. If it is lower, we sell the stock at the current price and move to the next potential buy point.

class Solution:

4. **Adjust Buy and Sell Points**:
