### Approach

2. **Indegree Tracking**: Track the number of dependencies (indegree) each recipe has. Only recipes with all dependencies resolved (indegree of 0) are processed.
3. **Topological Sorting**: Use a queue to process recipes in topological order, ensuring that each recipe is only processed once all its dependencies are met.
4. **Ingredient Availability Check**: For each recipe, check if all its ingredients are available either from the initial supplies or from previously made recipes.
