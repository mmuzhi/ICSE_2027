   - **Sum Calculation**: For each element, multiply its value by the number of subarrays where it is the minimum (calculated using the left and right boundaries) and accumulate the result.

4. **Complexity Analysis**: The algorithm processes each element twice (once for left boundaries and once for right boundaries), resulting in a time complexity of O(n). The space complexity is O(n) due to the stack usage.
