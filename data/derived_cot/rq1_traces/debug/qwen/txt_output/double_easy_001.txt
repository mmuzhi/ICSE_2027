2. **Calculate Target Sum**: If the total sum is divisible by 3, we compute the target sum for each part (i.e., one-third of the total sum).

class Solution:
    def canThreePartsEqualSum(self, arr: List[int]) -> bool:
        total = sum(arr)
        if total % 3 != 0:
