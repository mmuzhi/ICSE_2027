3. **Binary Indexed Tree (BIT) Setup**: We use a BIT to keep track of the positions assigned during the transformation. The BIT helps in efficiently querying the number of characters assigned up to a certain position.
4. **Transformation Validation**: For each character in the source string `s`, we assign the next available position from the corresponding deque in `t`.
