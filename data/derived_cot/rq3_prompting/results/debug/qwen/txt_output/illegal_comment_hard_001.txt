### Approach

If they are the same, we extend the count of slices ending at `i-1` by one. Otherwise, we start a new slice of length 3 starting at `i-2`, `i-1`, and `i`.

2. **Dynamic Programming Setup**: Initialize the `dp` array with zeros. The first valid arithmetic slice starts at index 2 (0-based), so `dp[2]` is set to 1.
3. **Iteration**: For each subsequent element, check the condition for forming a new arithmetic slice. Update the `dp` array and accumulate the total count of slices.
