2. **Iterate Through the Flowerbed**: For each spot in the extended flowerbed (excluding the added edges), check if it and its immediate neighbors are empty (0). If they are, plant a flower (mark it as 1) and decrement the count of flowers to plant (`n`).
3. **Check Result**: After processing all spots, if the remaining count of flowers to plant (`n`) is zero or less, return True; otherwise, return False.
