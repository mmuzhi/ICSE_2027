   - For `addBinary`, we process each bit from the least significant to the most significant, compute the sum with carry, and append the least significant bit of the sum to the result. The carry is updated by right-shifting the sum.
   - For `addNegabinary`, the sum is computed similarly, but the carry is updated by negating the right-shifted sum. Trailing zeros are removed from the end of the result to ensure the output is in the correct format.

4. **Complexity Analysis**:

    def addBinary(self, A, B):
