To solve this problem, we need to find the most common word in a given paragraph after normalizing the text and excluding banned words. The solution involves several steps: normalizing the text by converting it to lowercase and removing punctuation, splitting the text into words, filtering out banned words, and then counting the occurrences of each word to determine the most common one.

### Approach
1. **Normalize the Paragraph**: Convert the entire paragraph to lowercase to ensure case insensitivity. Replace all specified punctuation marks (.,!?;') with spaces to separate words properly.
