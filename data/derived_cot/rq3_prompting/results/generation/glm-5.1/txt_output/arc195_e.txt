**Key Insight:** For each edge i (connecting vertex i to parent P_i with weight A_i), it contributes to the distance between u and v if and only if exactly one of u, v is in the subtree rooted at i.

**Computing Probabilities:**
- P(vertex a is ancestor of vertex b) = 1/a for a < b (can be proven by induction)
- For i < u < v: P(i is ancestor of both u and v) = (i + u - 1)/(u·i²)

This gives us:
- For i < u < v: P(exactly one of u,v in subtree of i) = 2(u-1)(i-1)/(u·i²)
- For i = u < v: P = (u-1)/u
- For u < i < v: P = 1/i  
- For i = v: P = 1
- For i > v: P = 0

**Formula for answer:**
Answer = (N-1)! × [Σᵢ₌₂ᵘ⁻¹ Aᵢ·2(u-1)(i-1)/(u·i²) + Aᵤ·(u-1)/u + Σᵢ₌ᵤ₊₁ᵛ⁻¹ Aᵢ/i + Aᵥ]
