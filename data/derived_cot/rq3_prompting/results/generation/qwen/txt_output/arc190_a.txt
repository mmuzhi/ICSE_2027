The goal is to cover the entire sequence with 1s using the minimal number of operations (each Operation 1 or 2 costs 1, while Operation 0 costs 0).

2. **Key Insights**:
   - **Single Operation Check**: If any operation covers the entire sequence (i.e., the interval is [1, N]), use that operation alone.
