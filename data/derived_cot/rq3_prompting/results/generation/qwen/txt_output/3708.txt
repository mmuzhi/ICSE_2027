    def zigzagTraversal(self, grid: List[List[int]]) -> List[int]:
        m = len(grid)

   - **Filter Alternate Cells**: From the generated sequence, retain only those cells at even indices (0-based) in the sequence.

3. **Algorithm Selection**: The algorithm first constructs the entire zigzag traversal sequence by processing each row in the specified direction. Then, it filters the sequence to include only the cells at even indices (0, 2, 4, etc.).
