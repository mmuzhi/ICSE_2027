2. **Connected Components**: Using a Union-Find (Disjoint Set Union, DSU) data structure, we determine the connected components of the graph. The intervals are sorted by their right endpoints to efficiently find disjoint intervals and union them into connected components.
3. **Query Processing**: For each query, we check if the vertices are in the same connected component. If they are not, the answer is -1.
