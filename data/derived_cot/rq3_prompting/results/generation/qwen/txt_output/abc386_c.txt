   This implies an insertion operation might be needed. Traverse both strings to find the first mismatch. If the remaining characters match after skipping one character in `T`, the answer is "Yes". Otherwise, "No".

5. **Case 5: Length difference is more than one**  
   It's impossible to transform `S` into `T` with one operation, so the answer is "No".
